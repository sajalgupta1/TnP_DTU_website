About Us

The Training & Placement Cell is the department run by the academics head of DTU that aims at manifesting the talent & spirit of the young students from all corners of DTU. Keeping this motto in mind the TNP,DTU is reaching out to all the students across DTU to promote and provide them with the right opportunity by providing them with such a large pool of company from which they can select thier dream Company.
A student would be selected by companies based on his/her "Resume", Talent and by their ability to solve problems and by looking at their skills and dedication to work for their organization.
Training and Placement Cell is always ready to help all the students across DTU and try to get them all a good offer so that they can work for their happiness as well as can maintain the aroma of success of DTU in all the corners of world.
